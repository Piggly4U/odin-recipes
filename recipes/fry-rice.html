Piggly's Fry Rice 4U

Piggly's Steamy Fry Rice 4U is a savory dish that will have you limping back for fourths.

Ingredients

    (for 1 serving)

  • 1/2 bag French Fries (any-brand)
  • 1 cup rice
  • 1 cup mixed veg
  • 1/2 lb beef or choice of meat
  • 1 tbs butter
  • 3 tbs salt
  • 1 tbs pepper
  1. Preheat oven to 425 F and add French fries for 15-17 minutes
  2. Bring 1 cup water to a boil while fries are heating
  3. Add 1 tbs of salt and butter to boiling water
  4. Add 1 cup of rice to water and let boil for 1 minute
  5. Turn off stove and cover rice for 15 minutes
  6. Fry 1/2 lb of meat on large skillet or wok until internal temp of 150 F
  7. Mix finished fries, rice and choice of meat
  8. Add 2 tbs salt and papper
  9. Add any optional seasonings of your liking
  10. enjoy
