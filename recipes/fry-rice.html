Piggly's Fry Rice 4U

Piggly's Steamy Fry Rice 4U is a savory dish that will have you limping back for fourths.

Ingredients:

  • 12 ounces frozen french fries (about 3/4 of the bag)
  • 14 (1/2 oz) canned diced tomatoes with chipotle
  • 1 (1/4 lbs) sirloin tip steak
  • 1 red onion
  • 2 tbs chopped cilantro
  • 3 cloves garlic
  • 1/4 cup diced pickled jalapeno
  • Rice

Marinade

  • 1/4 cup red wine vinegar
  • 2 tbs soy sauce
  • 1 teaspoon cumin
  • 1 teaspoon coriander
  • Salt
  • Pepper

Instructions:

  1. Slice the steaks into thin strips and place in the marinade.
  2. Time the starting of the rice so it will b ready when the beef is.
  3. Thinly slice the onion, dice the garlic, chop the cilantro and jalapenos.
  4. Cook the fries to the package instructions.
  5. Start the onions in some olive oil and cook until softened, about 3 or 4 minutes. Add the jalepenos and tomatoes. Cook 3 minutes. Add the fries, mix and remove from heat.
  6. Serve over rice and garnish with cilantro.
  7. Enjoy!
